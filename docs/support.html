HSK Master Support

Thank you for using HSK Master. Please refer to the FAQ below for common questions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is HSK Master?
HSK Master is a vocabulary learning app for Chinese Proficiency Test (HSK) preparation. It helps you effectively learn essential vocabulary from HSK Level 1 to Level 6.
Q: What features does it provide?
  • Vocabulary learning by HSK Levels 1-6
  • Efficient memorization with flashcard function
  • Multi-language translation support (Korean, English, Japanese, etc.)
  • Learning progress tracking
  • Offline learning available
Q: Do I need an internet connection?
No. All vocabulary data is included in the app, so you can learn without an internet connection. Only ad display and in-app purchase features require an internet connection.
Q: Can I remove ads?
Yes. You can purchase ad removal for $1.99 within the app. Once purchased, ads will be permanently removed.
Q: My learning progress disappeared.
Learning progress is saved on the device. It may be deleted if you uninstall the app or reset your device. We recommend backing up regularly.
Q: Can I use it on other devices?
Yes. Both Android and iOS are supported. However, learning progress is stored separately on each device, so cross-device synchronization is not supported.
Q: Are there plans to add new words?
We are continuously updating and improving content. More words and features will be added in future updates.
